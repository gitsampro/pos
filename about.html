About PrayasOS

PrayasOS is a community-driven operating system project focused on creating a lightweight, efficient, and user-friendly Linux distribution. Our team of passionate developers and contributors work together to build an OS that combines performance with simplicity.

Our Core Values

Security First

We prioritize user security and privacy in every aspect of our development process.

Community Driven

Our success is built on the strength and diversity of our global community.

Open Source

We believe in the power of open source and collaborative development.

Innovation

We constantly push boundaries to deliver cutting-edge technology solutions.
